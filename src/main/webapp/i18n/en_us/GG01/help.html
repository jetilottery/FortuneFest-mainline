Fortunefest

Celebrate and let the confetti fly in Fortunefest! Match numbers to win prizes. Instant wins are multiplied, up to 200x! Packed with two dazzling bonus rounds full of multipliers: spin the wheel to collect multipliers in the Super Spin Bonus or reveal and collect matching multipliers in the Mystery Match Bonus.

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Select the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Select the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Tickets

Displays the current number of tickets. Up to 20 tickets may be played at one time.

Select the minus button (–) to decrease the number of tickets.

Select the plus button (+) to increase the number of tickets.

Total Cost

Displays the total cost to play based on the number of tickets selected.

Select BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Match any Lucky Numbers to any of Your Tickets on purchased rows and win the corresponding prize. All matching prizes are added to the total win.

Reveal a Multiplier Symbol to instantly win the revealed prize times the multiplier amount. Multipliers of 2x, 5x, 10x, 20x, 50x, 100x, 200x may be awarded.

Find a Mystery Box Symbol to trigger one of two mystery bonuses. The Mystery Box opens to reveal either the Super Spin Bonus or the Mystery Match Bonus.

In the Super Spin Bonus, spin the wheels to reveal multipliers. Revealed multipliers are accumulated. When the outer wheel lands on COLLECT, the bonus ends. Ticket cost is multiplied by the accumulated multipliers for a bonus win.

In the Mystery Match Bonus, reveal rows of multipliers. Reveal three matching multiplier values in any one row, to win the corresponding multiplier. It is possible to win on more than one row. Ticket cost is multiplied by accumulated multipliers for a bonus win.

The game ends when all Lucky Numbers, Your Tickets, and any awarded bonus games are revealed.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by selecting the message.

Reveal All

Select REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game's outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.

In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable.
